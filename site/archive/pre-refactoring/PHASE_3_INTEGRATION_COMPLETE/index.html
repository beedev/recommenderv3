    Preview vs Regular Search¶

    PREVIEW Search (Proactive Display): - When: Automatic display after component selection - Limit: 5 products (preview_limit from state_config.json) - Purpose: Quick preview of next compatible products - Example: After selecting PowerSource, automatically show 5 compatible Feeders

    REGULAR Search (User-Requested): - When: User explicitly requests products - Limit: 10 products (search_limit from state_config.json) - Purpose: Comprehensive product search - Example: User asks "show me feeders"
